lasagna

Making perfect homemade lasagna doesn’t have to be tedious. This top-rated easy lasagna recipe comes together quickly with a relatively short ingredient list.

Easy lasagna Ingredient

  • Beef
  • spaghetti
  • cheese
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna
  • water ½ cup

Steps for Making lasagna

  1. Beef: This easy lasagna starts with ground beef. You can use ground turkey for a lighter option.
  2. spaghetti sauce: Use store-bought or homemade spaghetti sauce.
  3. Cheeses: You’ll need cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  4. Eggs: Eggs help bind the cheese mixture together. Plus, they lend moisture and richness.
  5. Seasonings: Season the easy lasagna with dried parsley, salt, and black pepper.
  6. Lasagna noodles: Of course, you’ll need lasagna noodles!
  7. Water: Pour ½ cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking.
